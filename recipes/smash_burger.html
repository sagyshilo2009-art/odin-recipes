the best smash burger recipe:

description:

The smash burger is a fan favorite thanks to its crispy edges, juicy center, and bold flavor. Unlike thick burgers, smash burgers are flattened on a hot griddle for maximum browning and a crave-worthy crust. Simple, fast, and absolutely delicious.

ingredients list:

  • 250g (1/2 lb) ground beef (80/20 fat ratio is best)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 2 burger buns
  • 2 slices of American cheese (or cheddar)
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced (optional)
  • Pickles, lettuce, tomato (optional toppings)
  • Butter or oil for the pan
  • Burger sauce (optional — recipe below)

Optional Burger Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp mayo
  • 1 tsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 tsp pickle juice or relish
  • Mix together and spread on the buns!

Steps:

  1. Divide the beef into 4 equal balls (don’t overwork them). Keep them cold until cooking.
  2. Heat a pan or griddle. Butter the inside of the buns and toast until golden. Set aside.
  3. On a hot skillet or griddle, place 2 beef balls. Use a spatula (with parchment paper or foil) to smash them flat. Season with salt and pepper. Cook for about 2 minutes until the edges are crispy.
  4. Flip the burgers, add a slice of cheese to each, and cook for another 1–2 minutes until the cheese melts.
  5. Stack two patties per burger for a double smash. Add to the toasted bun with your favorite toppings and sauce.
  6. Enjoy with fries, a cold drink, or just by itself — it's that good.

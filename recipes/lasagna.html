lasagna recipe



description:

This classic homemade lasagna recipe features layers of tender pasta, rich and hearty meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese, baked to golden perfection. It's the ultimate comfort food—perfect for family dinners, special occasions, or just when you need a warm, satisfying meal.

Tender lasagna noodles are layered with a savory blend of ground beef and Italian sausage simmered in a tomato-based marinara sauce. Between the layers, creamy béchamel sauce adds a silky texture, while generous amounts of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese provide that irresistible cheesy pull in every bite. Baked until bubbling and golden, this lasagna delivers bold, comforting flavors in every layer.


Lasagna Ingredient List (Serves 6–8)

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2–3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz / 796 ml) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Optional: red pepper flakes or a splash of red wine

For the Ricotta Mixture:

  • 1 ½ cups (375g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: chopped parsley or spinach

Other Layers:

  • 9–12 lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup (50g) grated Parmesan cheese (for topping)

Optional:

  • Fresh basil for garnish
  • Extra mozzarella or béchamel sauce for richness

Classic Lasagna Recipe – Step-by-Step Guide

1. Cook the meat sauce:

  1. Heat a bit of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and cook until soft (2–3 minutes).
  3. Add garlic and cook 1 minute more.
  4. Add the ground meat. Cook until browned and no longer pink.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, herbs, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

2. Boil the noodles (Skip if using no-boil noodles):

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles until al dente (firm but cooked).
  3. Drain and lay them flat on a towel so they don't stick.

3. Mix the cheese filling:

  1. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix until smooth.

4. Preheat oven:

  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

5. Assemble the lasagna:

  1. Layer 1 (bottom): Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
  2. Add a layer of noodles.
  3. Spread ⅓ of the ricotta mixture over noodles.
  4. Sprinkle some mozzarella.
  5. Add more meat sauce.
  6. Repeat: Noodles → Ricotta → Mozzarella → Sauce
  7. Finish with a top layer of noodles, meat sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan.

6. Bake:

  1. Cover the dish with foil (tip: spray the underside with oil so it doesn’t stick to cheese).
  2. Bake for 25 minutes covered.
  3. Remove foil and bake 15–20 more minutes until bubbly and golden.

7. Rest and serve:

  • Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing. This helps it set.
